Social problems

Human society and culture are the fundamental aspects ofhuman life.Besides the many beautiful aspects of society,there are some bad activities and attitudes.The bad activities and practices which harm justice,development and prosperity in the society are social problems and evils.Some social problems of our society are based on traditional practices and supersticious beliefs.Imported cultures and influence have also created social probems in our society.Social problems and evils are the major obstacles to balance development and social justice.Therefore,the identification of the causes of these problems,control,and abolishment is very essential.

Some of the social problems preavaling in our society are:-
  1. Untouchability.
  2. Dowry System.
  3. Corruption.
  4. Drug Abuse.
  5. Human traffivking.
  6. Alcoholism.
  7. Gender discrimination.
  8. Extravagent expenditure.
  9. Polygamy.

Some of the main causes of these problems are:-

  • Extreme poverty and economic problem.
  • Caste,class and ereligious based discrimination.
  • Lawlessness.
  • Illiteracy.
  • Limited acess to education and opportunity.
  • Conservative thoughts and superstitious beliefs.
  • Over population.
  • Easy access to tobacco,cigarette,drugs,etc.
  • influence of the bad aspects of the foreign cultures
